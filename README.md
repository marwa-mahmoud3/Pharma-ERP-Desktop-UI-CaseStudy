## üåü Context & Scope
This project was my second professional collaboration with the same ITI instructor. My role was to architect the **User Interface and Experience (UI/UX)** for the entire ERP system, ensuring a seamless, consistent, and professional look across all business modules.

### **Why this is a "Senior" UI Project:**
* **Complexity**: Designing interfaces for high-density data (Financials, Inventory, HR).
* **Consistency**: Managing a unified design system across multiple modules.
* **Prototyping to Implementation**: Converting complex business requirements into functional DevExpress layouts.

### üìä Financials & Business Intelligence
* **Accounting Dashboards**: Clean layouts for financial tracking and reporting.

---

## üß† Design Philosophy & Challenges

### 1. High-Density Data Visualization
* **Challenge**: How to display massive amounts of pharmacy data without overwhelming the user.
* **Solution**: Implementing DevExpress Layout Managers and Tabbed interfaces to categorize information logically.

### 2. Speed & Accessibility
* **Challenge**: Pharmacists need to work fast.
* **Solution**: Designed keyboard-centric workflows and "Fast Search" components to reduce user friction.
